Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?
Navigate to the Projects page and click the "Create New Project" button. Fill in the project details including name, description, start date, end date, and assign a project manager. Click "Create Project" to save.
How do I assign tasks to team members?
Go to the Tasks page, select a task, and use the "Assign To" dropdown to select a team member. You can also create new tasks with assignments directly from the project detail page.
How do I integrate with Google Calendar?
Go to Integrations page, find the Google Calendar card, and click "Connect". Follow the OAuth flow to authorize access. Once connected, project deadlines and task due dates will sync automatically.
How do I export reports?
Navigate to the Reports page, select your desired date range and filters, then click the "Export Report" button. You can export in PDF, Excel, or CSV formats.
How do I reset a user's password?
As an admin, go to Users page, find the user, click the edit button, and enter a new password. The user will need to change their password on next login if you enable that option.
